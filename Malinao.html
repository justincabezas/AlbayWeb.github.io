Malinao Municipality

Malinao is a coastal municipality in the province of Albay.The municipality has a land area of 107.50 square kilometers or 41.51 square miles which constitutes 4.17% of Albay's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 47,395. This represented 3.45% of the total population of Albay province, or 0.78% of the overall population of the Bicol Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 441 inhabitants per square kilometer or 1,142 inhabitants per square mile. Malinao has a total of 29 barangays.The household population of Malinao in the 2015 Census was 45,210 broken down into 9,765 households or an average of 4.63 members per household.The population of Malinao grew from 12,437 in 1903 to 47,395 in 2020, an increase of 34,958 people over the course of 117 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 0.96%, or an increase of 2,094 people, from the previous population of 45,301 in 2015.The municipal center of Malinao is situated at approximately 13° 24' North, 123° 42' East, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 7.6 meters or 24.9 feet above mean sea level.

Pio Duran Municipality

Pio Duran, formerly known as Malacbalac, is a coastal municipality in the province of Albay. The municipality has a land area of 133.70 square kilometers or 51.62 square miles which constitutes 5.19% of Albay's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 49,070. This represented 3.57% of the total population of Albay province, or 0.81% of the overall population of the Bicol Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 367 inhabitants per square kilometer or 951 inhabitants per square mile. Pio Duran has a total of 33 barangays. The household population of Pio Duran in the 2015 Census was 46,673 broken down into 9,467 households or an average of 4.93 members per household. The population of Pio Duran grew from 26,443 in 1970 to 49,070 in 2020, an increase of 22,627 people over the course of 50 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 1.05%, or an increase of 2,377 people, from the previous population of 46,693 in 2015. The municipal center of Pio Duran is situated at approximately 13° 3' North, 123° 27' East, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 17.7 meters or 58.2 feet above mean sea level.

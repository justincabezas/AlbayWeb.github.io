Daraga Municipality

Daraga, formerly known as Locsin, is a landlocked municipality in the coastal province of Albay. The municipality has a land area of 118.64 square kilometers or 45.81 square miles which constitutes 4.61% of Albay's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 133,893. This represented 9.74% of the total population of Albay province, or 2.20% of the overall population of the Bicol Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 1,129 inhabitants per square kilometer or 2,923 inhabitants per square mile. The household population of Daraga in the 2015 Census was 126,022 broken down into 27,648 households or an average of 4.56 members per household. Daraga Municipality has a total of 54 barangays.

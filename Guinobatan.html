Guinobatan Municipality

Guinobatan is a landlocked municipality in the coastal province of Albay.The municipality has a land area of 244.43 square kilometers or 94.37 square miles which constitutes 9.49% of Albay's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 85,786. This represented 6.24% of the total population of Albay province, or 1.41% of the overall population of the Bicol Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 351 inhabitants per square kilometer or 909 inhabitants per square mile. Guinobatan has a total of 44 barangays. The household population of Guinobatan in the 2015 Census was 82,237 broken down into 18,239 households or an average of 4.51 members per household. According to the 2015 Census, the age group with the highest population in Guinobatan is 5 to 9, with 8,961 individuals. Conversely, the age group with the lowest population is 80 and over, with 819 individuals. Combining age groups together, those aged 14 and below, consisting of the young dependent population which include infants/babies, children and young adolescents/teenagers, make up an aggregate of 31.94% (26,309). Those aged 15 up to 64, roughly, the economically active population and actual or potential members of the work force, constitute a total of 61.75% (50,858). Finally, old dependent population consisting of the senior citizens, those aged 65 and over, total 6.31% (5,194) in all.

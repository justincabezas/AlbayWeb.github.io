Ligao Municipality

Ligao is a coastal component city in the province of Albay.The city has a land area of 246.75 square kilometers or 95.27 square miles which constitutes 9.58% of Albay's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 118,096. This represented 8.59% of the total population of Albay province, or 1.94% of the overall population of the Bicol Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 479 inhabitants per square kilometer or 1,240 inhabitants per square mile. Ligao has a total of 55 barangays. The household population of Ligao in the 2015 Census was 111,241 broken down into 22,552 households or an average of 4.93 members per household.The population of Ligao grew from 17,687 in 1903 to 118,096 in 2020, an increase of 100,409 people over the course of 117 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 1.24%, or an increase of 6,697 people, from the previous population of 111,399 in 2015.The city center of Ligao is situated at approximately 13° 14' North, 123° 32' East, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 51.0 meters or 167.3 feet above mean sea level.

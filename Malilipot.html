Malilipot Municipality

Malilipot is a coastal municipality in the province of Albay.The municipality has a land area of 44.13 square kilometers or 17.04 square miles which constitutes 1.71% of Albay's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 40,857. This represented 2. 97% of the total population of Albay province, or 0.67% of the overall population of the Bicol Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 926 inhabitants per square kilometer or 2,398 inhabitants per square mile. Malilipot has a total of 18 barangays.The household population of Malilipot in the 2015 Census was 37,761 broken down into 8,104 households or an average of 4.66 members per household.The population of Malilipot grew from 5,501 in 1903 to 40,857 in 2020, an increase of 35,356 people over the course of 117 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 1.66%, or an increase of 3,072 people, from the previous population of 37,785 in 2015.The municipal center of Malilipot is situated at approximately 13° 19' North, 123° 44' East, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 11.2 meters or 36.6 feet above mean sea level.

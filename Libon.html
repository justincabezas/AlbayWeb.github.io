Libon Municipality

Libon is a coastal municipality in the province of Albay.The municipality has a land area of 235.70 square kilometers or 91.00 square miles which constitutes 9.15% of Albay's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 75,073. This represented 5.46% of the total population of Albay province, or 1.23% of the overall population of the Bicol Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 319 inhabitants per square kilometer or 825 inhabitants per square mile. Libon has a total of 47 barangays.The household population of Libon in the 2015 Census was 75,165 broken down into 15,528 households or an average of 4.84 members per household.The population of Libon grew from 4,616 in 1903 to 75,073 in 2020, an increase of 70,457 people over the course of 117 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a negative growth rate of 0.03%, or a decrease of 99 people, from the previous population of 75,172 in 2015.The municipal center of Libon is situated at approximately 13° 18' North, 123° 26' East, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 17.9 meters or 58.6 feet above mean sea level.
